Itinerary

Day by Day

Day 1 · Thu May 14

Arrive & Settle In

Fly Zurich → Marseille (1h25). Drive 1h to Bonnieux. Pool.

Dinner: La Trinquette, Gordes

Day 2 · Fri May 15

Camargue Horseback

3h beach ride through marshlands and Mediterranean coast. Pool afternoon.

Dinner: JU Bonnieux (Michelin)

Day 3 · Sat May 16

Village Exploration

Lourmarin chateau & boutiques. Menerbes walk. Maison de la Truffe et du Vin.

Dinner: Cafe Gaby, Lourmarin

Fishing Day

Half-day sport fishing from Cassis along the Calanques coast. Mediterranean catch.

Dinner: Le Numero 9, Lourmarin

Day 5 · Mon May 18

Gordes & Roussillon

Senanque Abbey, Gordes village, Roussillon ochre trail hike. Pool.

Dinner: L'Estellan, Gordes

Day 6 · Tue May 19

UNESCO Day Trip

Les Baux castle & Carrieres de Lumieres. Avignon: Palais des Papes.

Dinner: Restaurant Sevin (Michelin)

Day 7 · Wed May 20

Spa Day & Sunset

Pool morning. Couples spa at Coquillade. Vineyard sunset dinner.

Dinner: La Bastide de Marie

Day 8 · Thu May 21

Last Day

Market shopping at L'Isle-sur-la-Sorgue or Gordes. Final pool. Villa cook.

Dinner: Cook at home
